üîß What We Actually Built

1. The "Copy Machine" (Log Replication)

What it does: When the leader writes something new, it automatically copies it to all followers

Real-world analogy: Like a photocopier that makes copies for everyone

Technical term: add_entry() method

2. The "Progress Tracker" (Follower Tracking)

What it does: The leader keeps track of what each follower has copied

Real-world analogy: Like a teacher keeping track of which students have completed their homework

Technical terms: next_index and match_index

3. The "Safety Check" (Majority Commitment)

What it does: Only "finalizes" entries when most people have them

Real-world analogy: Like a group decision that only becomes official when most people agree

Technical term: _update_commit_index()

4. The "Action Taker" (State Machine Application)

What it does: Actually does something with the finalized entries

Real-world analogy: Like actually buying the milk after everyone agrees it's needed

Technical term: _apply_committed_entries()
